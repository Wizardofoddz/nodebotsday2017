## Bot connecting.

* service Id: `bada5555-e91f-1337-a49b-8675309fb099`

* digital Characteristic `2a56`

* analog Characteristic `2a58`

more details [here](https://github.com/monteslu/ble-io/blob/master/service.md)


## directional movement

* Right wheel forward:
digital: `[8,1]` and `[7,0]`

* Left wheel forward:
digital: `[5,1]` and `[4,0]`


* Right wheel reverse:
digital: `[8,0]` and `[7,1]`

* Left wheel reverse:
digital: `[5,0]` and `[4,1]`


## speed

* Right wheel full speed:
analog: `[9,255]`

* Left wheel full speed:
analog: `[3,255]`


* Right wheel STOP:
analog: `[9,0]`

* Left wheel STOP:
analog: `[3,0]`
